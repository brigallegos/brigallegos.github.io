what happens when the fog lifts





my 26th birthday was going to be extremely depressing that year

i thought about joining the 27 club a year early, joking...kinda

i had no plans, no money and not a lot of friends

so i did what any self respecting 20 something year old would do on their birthday

i went to a bar and drank alone

but only kind of alone





my best friend was working

honestly i had kind of neglected the friendship because i was severely depressed

and kind of incapable of the upkeep of friendships at the time

but here's the thing i love about this man

regardless how long we go without seeing each other or hanging out

he will always come to me with the warmest welcomes and the biggest hugs

i don't think he knows it

but he kind of saved my life that night

because i really wanted to do something stupid and final





so fast forward a few weeks after becoming a regular at this bar

i started working there

i meet some more of the best humans i've ever met

and something happened





the fog started lifting

and not just lifting, but like vaporizing

not just because i had a fun new job

or that i wasn't struggling financially

but i had found my people

and i started to actually like myself





it was safe to be me





new chapter and new loss
