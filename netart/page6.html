how could it get any worse

well...





the year that came after my grandmother's death was possibly the worst year of my life

like for real...hard as fuck

i had to move out of the only home i ever knew 7 months later

and i bounced around a lot





i had roommates steal from me

money, clothes, shoelaces

i didn't trust a lot of people then





i checked myself into the er a few times

the gaps between self harm and attempts to end my life were closing in

i totaled my car

i was broke, depressed, grieving

and worst of all i felt so alone





"it feels like walking through a dense forest in thick fog

it scares me

in years prior, there was safety

i had time and a place to catch my breath

it would feel like the fog would clear and I could see the next steps

there was home

this is the first time in my life there is no safety net

there is no back up plan, there is no place that feels like home

i'm tired of feeling lost

the fog hasn't cleared in a while and sometimes i'm scared it never will"

-feb 27 2019-





"i'm exhausted and i don't know if it's possible to keep pushing forward

it feels too dark to see the light, like there is no light

like there's no way out"

-aug 18 2018-





i felt so lost and hopeless

and i just kept pushing because that's all we can do sometimes

but fuck, were there days i really didn't want to

this went on until late 2019

riding the line between pushing forward and giving up





and then on my 26th birthday i visited a friend

at the bar he tends

and i didn't know it then

but that night changed everything





the fog begins to lift
