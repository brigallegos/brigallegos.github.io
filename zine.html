final project - g00d grief zine





for my final project, i complied a zine made with digital collage, photoshop, photography, illustration, and glitch data moshing, through audacity. the subject centers around grief and is an expansion of my net art project. i discuss grief in terms of grieving the place i have called home all my life, san jose. i have seen this place change and people be displaced over the last three decades. this piece brings attention to the conversation around gentrification and displacement in our city.
