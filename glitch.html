glitch art is the practice of using digital or analog errors
for aesthetic purposes by either
corrupting digital data or physically manipulating electronic devices.
using programs like audacity and through the editing of the image source code,
i was able to successfully glitch these photos
in an aesthetic and effective manner.
i have based this glitch series on a broken system
that is a part of our day to day lives.
this series comments on the aggressive nature of police
in spaces that are intended for voicing against injustices, mourning loss, and coming together as a community.
the original photos were taken at the george floyd protest in june 2020 in downtown san jose.
