losing her...



summer 2017 right after leaving a shitty job

my life fell apart

my family tore apart because of me

majors mistakes were made

things some believe are unforgivable

trust was shattered

and i ultimately wanted to end my life





my grandmother was the rock in our family

when she got really sick, i committed all of my time to her care

i would have done it regardless of the turmoil i had created

but this was my only way to make things right to her





so from the end of july, i stayed home

i prepared her meals and medications

i took her to all of her doctor visits

i took over all the household accounts and financial needs

i spent all my time with her





the routines, the hours of watching game shows, the sneaking of mcdonald's french fries

i think these were some of her best days

she wasn't always feeling the best or the strongest, but she was taken care of

she had someone to talk to and laugh with

who didn't make taking care of her feel like a burden

those are the days i miss the most

i miss her stories, her voice, her laugh





i wasn't ready for december 3rd 2017

she passed as i held her hand waiting for emt's to arrive





that morning, she remarked "what a beautiful day it is"

my dad says "i think she knew she was leaving us"

"that she was going to meet jesus"





i don't believe in god

but man, do i hope there's a heaven

because i know she'd be there





dare you continue to follow my loss?? it's a lot, you know...

or you can leave to find something less sad...i understand
