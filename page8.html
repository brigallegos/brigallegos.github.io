and with a new you comes the loss of what no longer serves you





by no means was this new season easy

and we were entering a global pandemic

it's like the world has been grieving for two years straight

but while it felt like the world was being set on fire

i started this change

and i began to grieve the girl i once was

that i was always this "new version" of me

at her stupidity, her inability to speak up for herself, at the way she people pleased

trying to bargain for the pieces of her i missed

sorry for her, that she deserved so much better and it wasn't granted to her
